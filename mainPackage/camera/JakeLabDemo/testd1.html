Enter the Arena

Rules

Welcome to Super Battle Arena! This is an always online multiplayer battle royale, where each player must fight to stay alive the longest. Each player spawns in a random location on the map, and must find weapons and armor on the ground. Once you have been killed, your score resets to 0 and you may enter again.

How to Play

Enter a name for yourself in the registration box above. Names are not unique, so feel free to be whoever you want. Afterwards, click Submit to create your character, and then enter the arena whenever you feel prepared.

Controls

Control your character using the WASD keys, and left click the mouse to shoot. You will automatically pick up anything you walk over.
