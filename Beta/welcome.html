RULES: Welcome to Super Battle Arena! This is an always online multiplayer battle royale, where each player must fight to stay alive the longest. Each player spawns in a random location on the map, and must pick up starts on the ground. If you collide with another player, whoever has the least amount of stars will be eliminated. Controls: Use W A S D to move, starts can be pickup up by running over them.
